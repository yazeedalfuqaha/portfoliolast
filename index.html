about me

Hi, I'm Yazeed, a passionate web developer and designer. I specialize in creating visually stunning and user-friendly websites.

As the Founder and CEO of OVIX IT, Yazeed Alfukaha leads a dynamic team specializing in web development, web design, and e-commerce solutions. With a strong foundation in modern technologies like Laravel, Yazeed brings innovative digital experiences to life through clean, responsive, and functional designs tailored to meet business goals. His technical expertise is complemented by a keen understanding of user-centric design, ensuring every project is intuitive and optimized across all devices.

Yazeed has successfully overseen the development of "The Goat and Route65 restaurant " a unique e-commerce platform for shoes, shirts, shorts, and bags. With a focus on delivering seamless shopping experiences, he has implemented advanced features such as multi-role systems, product detail customization, dynamic cart management, and responsive user interfaces.

As a dedicated problem-solver, Yazeed values precision and clarity in his code, consistently striving for solutions that combine technical excellence with business needs. His passion for technology, coupled with his entrepreneurial spirit, drives him to help businesses grow through tailored web solutions.
